About




An experienced developer who chose the IT industry after being inspired by Jarvis in the Iron Man movie. Since then I have been expanding my knowledge of programming languages, including React.js, CSS3, PHP, JavaScript, Node.js and Java. Right now, I am learning .Net, C# and C language in my current semester.

Today’s modern world is what it is because of the great innovative minds. I aspire to be one of them because my greatest passion is to give back to the community by using my coding skills. I am proud to share that I’ve already started to work towards this initiative by starting the Unite project, which is a social media site for my college. I noticed that most of the students are confused when they go to the next semester as they are confused about the courses and good instructors. If the Unite project is fully implemented, it would allow students to directly contact their seniors and request them for advice. It would also act as the hub for all the clubs at Sheridan.
